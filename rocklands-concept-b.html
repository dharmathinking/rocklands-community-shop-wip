Newsletter, social media, website — helping the community stay connected and informed.
🎉 Events
Chess afternoons, coffee mornings, seasonal events — helping bring the community together.
📋 Governance support

What you can expect from us

  • To be treated with dignity and respect at all times
  • To receive all the training you need to do your role safely and confidently
  • To be covered by the Society's Public Liability insurance throughout your volunteering
  • To have a clearly defined role, with support from our staff team
  • To raise any concerns through our formal channels
  • To be supported by our Equality & Diversity and Safeguarding policies

Intellectual property: Volunteers agree that any IP created during volunteering is assigned to Rocklands Community Shop Ltd, which retains ownership.

Who we are

About The Rocklands

A Community Benefit Society — owned by our members, run by our volunteers, trading for the good of this community. All profits stay right here in Rocklands.

Our mission

"We pledge to operate our business in an ethical, responsible, and sustainable way for the benefit of the local community and community projects as appropriate."

We source within 30 miles or across Norfolk wherever possible, and obtain meat only from Red Tractor assured suppliers. This isn't just policy — it reflects what we actually believe in.

Signed by all members of the Management Committee · 1 August 2013.

Our values

H
Honesty
Strong relationships built on trust — honest with our community, our suppliers and each other.
R
Responsibility
We take responsibility for our actions, always asking whether they're in the community's best interests.
Q
Quality
Providing and continually improving a quality service for everyone who relies on us.

Awareness
We understand how important this shop is — especially for elderly residents and those in isolated villages nearby.

Animal welfare, fair trade and social involvement — input from everyone in the community, without prejudice.

Membership

The Rocklands is a Community Benefit Society. Membership is how our community owns the shop. Members attend and vote at Members' Meetings and elect the Management Committee.

Privacy

  • We only collect personal data where genuinely necessary.
  • We will never share your information with third parties for marketing.
  • Your data is stored securely in line with UK GDPR.
  • You can request to see, correct or delete your data at any time.
  • We use cookies — you can accept or decline when you first visit.
  • Cardholder data is destroyed immediately after processing.

Complaints

We see every complaint as an opportunity to do better. Formal complaints must be submitted in writing to the Management Committee or Chair.

Stage 1

Acknowledged within 5 working days. Written response within 28 days.

Stage 2

Still not satisfied? Write to the Chair. A panel will respond within 28 days.

Address

Post: The Management Committee, Rocklands Community Shop Ltd, The Street, Rocklands, Norfolk, NR17 1HP. Or email via our — mark it 'Formal Complaint'.
