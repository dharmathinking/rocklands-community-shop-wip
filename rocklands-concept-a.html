Home / Our Suppliers
30 miles from our door 🗺️

Grown & made right here in Norfolk

Everything we can, we source within 30 miles. That means fresher food, fewer food miles, and support for the brilliant producers on our doorstep.

🌱

Our sourcing pledge

We pledge to source produce and products from sustainable sources grown, raised or made within a 30-mile radius of the shop, or in Norfolk, whenever possible. Shorter journeys mean fresher food, and buying local keeps money in our community.

Our producers 🥩🍦🍞

DJ Barnard's Meats
📍 Attleborough · ~8 miles

A brilliant local butcher providing fresh, dry-aged beef and lamb. All meat is assured under the Red Tractor scheme — because animal welfare matters to us.

🏷️ Red Tractor Assured
Dann's Farm
📍 Shipdham · ~5 miles

Just down the road! Dann's Farm produces amazing ice cream and lollies from their own dairy herd. The elderflower flavour is a summer favourite — grab it while it lasts!

🌿 5 miles away
Hingham Bakery
📍 Hingham · ~8 miles

Up at 5am baking so you don't have to be! Fresh sourdough, seeded bloomers and pastries delivered to us every single morning. On the shelves by 8:30am — don't hang about on Sundays!

🍞 Fresh every day

Whatever your skills, whatever your schedule, there's likely a role that suits you. It's a great way to meet people, give something back, and be part of something that really matters to this community.

Where you could help 🙌

🛒 Shop floor & till
Serving customers, running the till, keeping shelves stocked and tidy. The friendly face of Rocklands Market!
☕ Café team
Making drinks, serving food and cake, keeping the café warm and welcoming. A great social role.
📦 Stock & deliveries
Receiving deliveries, checking and rotating stock, keeping everything fresh and well-organised.
📣 Comms & social media
Helping with the newsletter, social media, website updates and keeping the community in the loop.
🎉 Events
Helping organise community events — chess afternoons, coffee mornings, seasonal celebrations and more.
📋 Governance & admin
Supporting the Management Committee with meetings, minutes and making sure everything runs smoothly.

What you can expect from us 💚

  • To be treated with dignity and respect at all times
  • To get all the training you need to do your role well and safely
  • To be covered by the Society's Public Liability insurance while you volunteer
  • To have a clearly defined role with support from our staff team
  • To raise any concerns through our formal channels — we always listen
  • To be supported by our Equality & Diversity and Safeguarding policies

Intellectual property: Volunteers agree that any IP created whilst volunteering is assigned to Rocklands Community Shop Ltd, which retains ownership.

Who we are 💚

About Rocklands Market

A Community Benefit Society — owned by our members, run by our volunteers, trading for the good of the whole community.

Our mission 🌿

"We pledge to operate our business in an ethical, responsible, and sustainable way for the benefit of the local community and community projects as appropriate."

We source within 30 miles or across Norfolk wherever we can. We only buy meat from Red Tractor assured suppliers who take animal welfare seriously.

Signed by all members of the Management Committee · Dated 1 August 2013.

Our values

H
Honesty
We build strong relationships on trust — speaking honestly with suppliers, community members and each other.
R
Responsibility
We take responsibility for our actions and always ask: is this in the best interests of our community?
Q
Quality
We aim to provide and continually improve a quality service for everyone who walks through our door.
A
Awareness
We know how much this shop means — especially to elderly residents and those in our more isolated villages.
E
Ethics
We believe in animal welfare, fair trade and social involvement — welcoming input from everyone, without prejudice.

Membership 🤝

As a Community Benefit Society, our members own the shop. Membership is how the community has a real say in how we're run. Members attend and vote at Members' Meetings and elect the Management Committee.

Want to become a member? 😊

Membership is open to everyone who supports our mission. Just get in touch and we'll send you the details.

Complaints 💬

We see every complaint as a chance to do better. Formal complaints must be submitted in writing — either by post or email — to the Management Committee or Chair.

Stage 1 — First look

We'll acknowledge within 5 working days and give you a written response within 28 days.

Stage 2 — Further review

Still not happy? Write to the Chair and a panel will respond within 28 days.
